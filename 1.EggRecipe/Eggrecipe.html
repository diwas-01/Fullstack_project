Egg Curry

This is a simple and tasty homemade egg curry recipe that can be enjoyed with rice or bread.

Ingredients

  • 4 boiled eggs
  • 2 onions
  • 2 tomatoes
  • Spices (turmeric, chili, cumin, salt).

Instructions

  1. Boil the eggs, peel them, and keep aside.
  2. Fry chopped onions until golden brown.
  3. Add tomatoes and spices, cook into a thick gravy.
  4. Add boiled eggs into the gravy and simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Serve hot with steamed rice or bread and butter.
